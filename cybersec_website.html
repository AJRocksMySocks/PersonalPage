SANS Work Study Program

What is SANS Work Study?

The SANS Work Study Program is an incredible opportunity to attend world-class cybersecurity training courses worth $6,000+ for FREE. In exchange for 20-25 hours of work during the event, you get access to premium SANS training, materials, and certification attempts.

🎯 Perfect For: Students, career changers, and professionals looking to advance their cybersecurity skills without the high cost of SANS training.

Program Benefits

Work study participants receive everything a paying student gets - course materials, virtual machines, practice exams, and access to the instructor and community - all while gaining valuable experience working SANS events.

What You Get

  • • Free SANS training course ($6,000+ value)
  • • All course materials and virtual machines
  • • Practice exams and labs
  • • Networking with cybersecurity professionals
  • • 4-month access to OnDemand content
  • • GIAC certification attempt included

Your Responsibilities

  • • 20-25 hours of work during the event
  • • Assist with registration and logistics
  • • Help distribute materials
  • • Support technical setup
  • • Provide customer service to attendees
  • • Maintain professional demeanor

Requirements

  • • Must be 18+ years old
  • • Available for entire event duration
  • • Professional attitude and appearance
  • • Reliable and punctual
  • • Some technical knowledge helpful
  • • Commitment to learning

Ready to Apply?

SANS Work Study positions fill up quickly. Applications are typically reviewed on a first-come, first-served basis.

Apply for SANS Work Study

Application Tips

  • • Apply early - positions fill quickly
  • • Choose courses aligned with your career goals
  • • Be flexible with location and dates
  • • Highlight any relevant experience
  • • Show genuine interest in cybersecurity
